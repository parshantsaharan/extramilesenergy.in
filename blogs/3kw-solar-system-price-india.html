A 3kW solar system generates around 12-15 units per day depending on sunlight hours, location, and weather conditions. In sunny states like Rajasthan, Gujarat, and Maharashtra, generation can be on the higher side.

Solar Subsidy for 3kW System

Under the government PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana, homeowners can receive subsidy up to ₹78,000 for rooftop solar installations of 3kW capacity.

Components of 3kW Solar System

  • Solar Panels: 6-8 panels of 400W-500W capacity
  • Solar Inverter: 3kW capacity (string inverter or hybrid inverter)
  • Mounting Structure: GI or aluminium structure for roof mounting
  • DC & AC Wiring: Solar cables, MC4 connectors, junction boxes
  • Net Meter: For exporting excess power to grid
  • Optional Battery Backup: If you want power during night/outages

Payback Period

💰 Investment Recovery: Most households recover their solar investment within 3-5 years.

After the payback period, electricity generated is practically free for the next 20-25 years. With rising electricity tariffs, the savings increase every year.

Example calculation for a household paying ₹8 per unit:

  • Monthly generation: 400 units
  • Monthly savings: ₹3,200
  • Yearly savings: ₹38,400
  • Payback period: ₹1.2 lakh ÷ ₹38,400 = ~3.1 years
