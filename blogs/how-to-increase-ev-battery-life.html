How to Increase Battery Life of Your EV

Published by Extra Miles Energy
Hisar, Haryana
Published: 29 January 2026 | Updated: 29 January 2026

Electric vehicles are revolutionizing transportation in India. With proper battery care techniques, you can significantly extend your EV's range, save money on replacements, and enhance overall performance. This comprehensive guide covers the essential practices for maximizing your EV battery lifespan.

1

Avoid Overcharging

  • Stop charging at 90–95% for daily use
  • Avoid overnight charging when possible
  • Use smart chargers with auto-cutoff
2

Avoid Deep Discharge

  • Recharge when battery reaches 20-30%
  • Never run battery down to 0%
  • Plan longer trips with charging stops
3

Smart Charging Practices

  • Always use certified OEM chargers
  • Limit fast charging to emergency situations
  • Follow manufacturer's charging guidelines
4

Temperature Control

  • Park in shade during hot days
  • Avoid direct rain exposure
  • Store in moderate temperature conditions
5

Smooth Driving Habits

  • Maintain steady speed when possible
  • Avoid harsh acceleration and braking
  • Use regenerative braking features
6

Regular Maintenance

  • Schedule annual battery health check
  • Inspect wiring and connectors regularly
  • Keep battery terminals clean
