üîã Battery Technology Difference

Lead Acid Battery

150+ years old technology, heavy weight, short life cycle, requires regular maintenance. Commonly used in traditional vehicles and inverters.

  • Heavy - 25-30 kg for 60V
  • 300-500 charge cycles
  • 6-8 hours charging time
  • Requires water topping

LiFePO4 Battery

Modern lithium technology, lightweight, long lifespan, maintenance-free. Specifically designed for electric vehicles with safety features.

  • Light - 10-12 kg for 60V
  • 3000+ charge cycles
  • 2-3 hours fast charging
  • Zero maintenance
