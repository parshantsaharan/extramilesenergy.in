‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. How long does lithium battery actually last for home use?

Our LiFePO4 batteries provide 7-10 years of reliable service with daily use (3000+ charge cycles). With proper usage and regular full charges, they can even last longer. Compare this to lead acid batteries that need replacement every 2-3 years.

Q2. Is lithium battery safe for indoor installation at home?

Absolutely safe. LiFePO4 chemistry is inherently stable and non-flammable. Our batteries include advanced BMS with temperature control, overcharge protection, short circuit protection, and cell balancing. They produce no toxic fumes and can be installed indoors safely.

Q3. Can I use lithium battery with my existing inverter?

Most modern inverters (after 2018) support lithium batteries. For older inverters, we provide compatibility check and may recommend a small settings adjustment or voltage calibrator. We test compatibility before delivery to ensure perfect operation.

Q4. What maintenance is required for home lithium batteries?

Zero regular maintenance. Unlike lead acid batteries that need monthly water topping and terminal cleaning, lithium batteries are completely maintenance-free. Just ensure they're kept in a dry place and avoid extreme temperatures for optimal performance.
