üîã Battery Cycle Kya Hota Hai?

Ek battery cycle ka matlab hota hai:

  • Battery 100% charge hui
  • Uske baad 0% ke paas discharge hui
  • Phir dobara full charge hui

Is poore process ko 1 cycle kaha jata hai.

Important Note: Agar aap battery ko 50% use karke charge karte ho, to wo 0.5 cycle mana jata hai. Isliye partial charging se battery life badhti hai.

  • Aap 4000 baar full charge-discharge kar sakte ho

  • Battery turant kharab nahi hoti
