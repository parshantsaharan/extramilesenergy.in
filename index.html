Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our batteries

What is the warranty period for your batteries?
We provide a 3-year warranty on our lithium battery packs. The warranty covers manufacturing defects and performance issues under normal usage conditions. Please refer to our warranty terms for detailed information.
How long does the battery last on a single charge?
Battery life depends on various factors including vehicle type, motor power, terrain, and load. Typically, our 60V 30Ah battery provides 60-80 km range for electric scooters, while 72V 45Ah battery can provide 100-130 km range for e-rickshaws.
Do you provide custom battery solutions?
Yes, we specialize in custom battery packs. We can design batteries according to your specific voltage, capacity, size, and application requirements. Contact us with your specifications for a customized solution.
How can I place an order?
You can place an order directly through WhatsApp by clicking the "Order Now" button on any product, or by calling us at +91-9991144903. We'll guide you through the order process, payment, and delivery details.
Do you provide installation support?
While we provide detailed installation instructions with each battery, for complex installations we can connect you with our authorized service partners in your city. We also offer remote guidance via video call if needed.
