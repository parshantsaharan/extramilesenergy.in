Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our batteries

What is the warranty period for your batteries?
We provide a 3-year warranty on our lithium battery packs. The warranty covers manufacturing defects and performance issues under normal usage conditions. Please refer to our warranty terms for detailed information about coverage and claim process.
How long does the battery last on a single charge?
Battery life depends on various factors including vehicle type, motor power, terrain, load, and driving style. Typically, our 60V 30Ah battery provides 60-80 km range for electric scooters, while 72V 45Ah battery can provide 100-130 km range for e-rickshaws under normal conditions.
Do you provide custom battery solutions?
Yes, we specialize in custom battery packs. We can design batteries according to your specific voltage, capacity, size, and application requirements. Contact us with your vehicle specifications, space availability, and power requirements for a customized solution.
How can I place an order?
You can place an order directly through WhatsApp by clicking the "Order on WhatsApp" button on any product, or by calling us at +91-9991144903. We'll guide you through the order process, payment options, delivery details, and installation support if needed.
Do you provide installation support?
While we provide detailed installation instructions with each battery, for complex installations we can connect you with our authorized service partners in your city. We also offer remote guidance via video call if needed. Basic installations can be done by following our step-by-step guide.
