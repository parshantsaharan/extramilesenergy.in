Our Story

Extra Miles Energy was founded with a vision to provide reliable, long-life lithium battery solutions for electric vehicles and solar systems in India. Based in Hisar, Haryana, we specialize in designing and manufacturing high-performance LiFePO₄ battery packs for EV scooters, e-rickshaws, solar systems, and custom applications.

Our Mission

To deliver safe, durable, and affordable lithium battery solutions with excellent after-sales support.

Our Vision

To become North India's most trusted LiFePO₄ battery manufacturer, supporting the growth of electric mobility and clean energy.
