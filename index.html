Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our batteries

What is the warranty period for your batteries?
We provide a 3-year warranty on our lithium battery packs. The warranty covers manufacturing defects and performance issues under normal usage conditions.
How long does the battery last on a single charge?
Battery life depends on various factors including vehicle type, motor power, terrain, and load. Typically, our 60V 30Ah battery provides 60-80 km range for electric scooters.
Do you provide custom battery solutions?
Yes, we specialize in custom battery packs. We can design batteries according to your specific voltage, capacity, size, and application requirements.
