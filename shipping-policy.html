🚚 Shipping Policy bulky items · special handling

Last Updated: March 2026 · Extra Miles Energy (extramilesenergy.in)
🔋 Extra Miles Energy specializes in bulky lithium batteries that require special handling and safe packaging. Please review our shipping charges carefully before placing your order.

📦 ₹500 - ₹1000

Shipping charges for bulky lithium battery orders (depending on weight, dimensions and destination).

✅ Free shipping on orders above ₹25,000 (all-India standard delivery).

1 Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends & holidays). Bulky items may require an additional day for safe packaging. You'll receive a confirmation once your order ships.

2 Shipping Charges

Due to the bulky nature and weight of lithium batteries, shipping charges are calculated as follows:

  • Standard shipping (bulky): ₹500 – ₹1000* depending on pincode and battery size.
  • Express shipping (bulky): ₹800 – ₹1500* for faster delivery (2-3 business days, wherever available).
  • Free shipping automatically applied on orders above ₹25,000 (standard delivery only).

* Exact charge will be shown at checkout. For heavy industrial batteries, we may contact you for exact quote.

3 Shipping Areas

We ship to all pincodes across India. However, some remote locations may have slightly higher charges or additional transit time due to battery transport regulations. We'll keep you informed.

4 Tracking

Once shipped, tracking ID will be shared via email/WhatsApp. You can track directly on the courier partner's website.

5 Delivery Timeline
  • Standard (bulky): 4-7 business days after processing.
  • Express (bulky): 2-4 business days (metro cities).

Remote / north-east regions may take 1-2 extra days.

6 Returns & Damaged Shipments

If your order arrives damaged during shipping, please contact us within 24 hours with photos of the package and product. Our support team will assist with replacement or resolution according to our return policy.

7 Delayed / Lost Orders

If your order is delayed beyond 10 business days, please contact us immediately for investigation. We'll track and resolve.
