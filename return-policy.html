🔋 At Extra Miles Energy, we provide high‑quality lithium batteries and energy products. Please read our return policy carefully before initiating a return.
1 Return Eligibility

Customers can request a return within 7 days of delivery if:

  • Product is damaged during shipping
  • Wrong product was delivered
  • Product has manufacturing defects
The product must be:
  • Unused and in original condition
  • Returned with original packaging
  • Returned with invoice or proof of purchase
2 Non-Returnable Items

✖ not eligible The following items cannot be returned:

  • Used batteries
  • Batteries damaged due to improper installation or misuse
  • Products damaged by water, short circuit, or physical damage
  • Customized battery packs
3 Return Process

To initiate a return, follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team within 7 days of delivery
  2. Share order number, photos, and issue details
  3. Our team will review the request and approve the return if eligible
