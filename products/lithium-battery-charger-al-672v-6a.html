Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can this charger charge a 60V lithium battery?
Yes, this charger outputs 67.2V which is the correct charging voltage for 60V lithium-ion battery packs.
Q: Is this charger suitable for LiFePO4 batteries?
No, this charger is designed for standard lithium-ion batteries (4.2V per cell). For LiFePO4 batteries (3.2V per cell), we recommend our 69.4V charger.
Q: What is the difference between 67.2V and 69.4V chargers?
67.2V chargers are for standard lithium-ion batteries (16 cells × 4.2V = 67.2V).
69.4V chargers are for LiFePO4 batteries (20 cells × 3.47V = 69.4V).
Please check your battery specifications before ordering.
Q: How long does it take to fully charge a battery?
Charging time depends on your battery capacity. For a 60V 30Ah battery, it takes approximately 5-6 hours (30Ah ÷ 6A = 5 hours).
